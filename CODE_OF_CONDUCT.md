# DytallixHQ Community Standards

This organization publishes security-sensitive infrastructure, cryptography,
and developer tooling. Discussions here need to stay technical, respectful,
and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, factual, and respectful.
- Focus on reproducible reports, concrete proposals, and verifiable evidence.
- Assume good faith, but challenge technical claims when the evidence is weak.
- Keep security-sensitive details private until the issue is fixed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or hateful conduct.
- Spam, promotion, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing exploit details for unfixed vulnerabilities in public issues or PRs.

## Scope

These standards apply in issues, pull requests, discussions tied to this
organization, and linked support channels used for repository collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these
standards. Repeated or severe violations may lead to repository or
organization-level restrictions.

For security issues, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of public issue
threads.
